About Cybervists

Empowering communities through responsible technology use, cybersecurity awareness, and inclusive digital education.

Who Are We?

We are a community interest organisation that empowers individuals and communities with digital literacy, cybersecurity awareness, and employment support. We aim to foster internet safety, inclusivity, and responsible digital citizenship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance digital literacy, promote responsible internet use and foster internet safety. We believe that by fostering a culture of support and inclusion, we can safeguard and digitally empower our community members to thrive.

Our Vision

We envision a future where everyone can use technology in a safe, responsible, mentally healthy way. A future where digital literacy is universal, and no one is left behind. By championing a balanced and mindful approach, we believe in a future where technology enhances life for all.

Ready to Join Our Mission?

Together, we can build a safer, more inclusive digital world for everyone.
